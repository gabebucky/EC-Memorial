A poem for Tom
The Roblox School Guy
(and Eurovision Superfan!)

Tom opened a Roblox school,
And thought he'd be so cool,
But somehow—twice—it had a fall,
Students ran from every hall!

The first time, Katie found a glitch,
Sent the teachers in a twitch.
The second time? Oh, what a mess—
Jack and George turned a quiz turned into utter distress!

But when he’s not in Roblox strife,
He’s got another love in life. Eurovision, oh, how grand!
With crazy songs and sparkly bands!

He cheers for songs with wild dances,
Watches in awe, lost in trances.
He even mimics moves at home,
A Eurovision star—right in his dome!

So here’s to Tom, who closes schools,
And cheers for Eurovision fools.
May your Ro-school thrive next time,
And Eurovision keep your shine!
